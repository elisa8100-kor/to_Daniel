Dear Cousin,

Hi! I hope you are doing well. Do you remember when we played chess together in Australia, sitting across from each other and taking turns? I still remember that time clearly.

I was thinking it would be nice to play chess with you again. This time, I was wondering if we could try correspondence chess. We can send one move at a time by message and take as long as we want to think.

We don’t need to hurry, and it doesn’t matter who wins. I just think it would be fun to play together again, even from far away.

If you would like to try, please let me know. I’m okay with either color.

Your cousin
